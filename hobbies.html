Travel

Fortunate to have visited 22 countries. I always have a grand time in Kuala Lumpur and I hope to visit the Merdeka 118 soon.
Taipei, home to the greatest museums in the world (even better than the Smithsonian), is remarkable and the 101 is phenomenal
building. Singapore, home to some of the greatest food in the world is the most modern place I've been to, and quite possibly
in the world. I still need to test this by visiting mainland China, specifically Shanghai/Hangzhou. Most of my current interests
in travel can be found in mainland China. Cities like Qingdao and Chongqing have an odd allure that I'd love to experience.
I'm also interested in visiting Kuwait and Saudi Arabia, especially after spending time in Abu Dhabi. Also, Namibia would be cool.

If I could recommend a travel place for someone, it would be Hong Kong by a mile. I solo traveled and spent 10 days there
and had a blast. The city is amazing and the universities were fun to explore. I'm currently looking at opportunities at
The University of Hong Kong, I had a blast exploring the campus and I love the neighboring streets. Maybe post undergrad?
